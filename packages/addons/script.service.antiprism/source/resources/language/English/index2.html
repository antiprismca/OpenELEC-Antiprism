    Configuring your browser for using with AntiPrism (Tor or I2P-based):

  • Open your proxy settings
  • Enter the IP address of your AntiPrism as the proxy address
  • Select port 1080, SOCKS5 proxy type for browsing via Tor, or port 4444/HTTP, port 4445/HTTPS for browsing I2P sites
  • Make sure, your AntiPrism "Access Control" settings permit accessing the proxy from your browser's IP address
  • Enjoy!
